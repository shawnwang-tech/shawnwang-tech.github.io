About Me

Shuo Wang is currently a graduate research assistant at School of System Science, Beijing Normal University under the supervision of Prof. Jiang Zhang. Prior to that, he was an algorithm engineer (deep learning) in ColorfulClouds Technology focusing on haze prediction. He received his M.S. degree in pattern recognition and intelligent system from Northeastern University, China, in 2014. His research interests include Complex Network, Graph Neural Networks (GNN), Spatio-Temporal forecasting, Haze Prediction.
